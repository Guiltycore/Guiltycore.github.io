  • Game project

    When I was studiying at EPITA in 2015-2016, the school
    gave us a project which was to create a video game.
    Soo, with 3 other people, we made a game named "The Mystery Circle"
    It's a puzzle game, where there is 7 room and a boss room.

    In my case I've done 2 rooms and multiplayer mode.
    The first room I've done is a reversed gravity room where the player
    Will have to make his way out by reversing the gravity throught
    a labyrinth.

    The second room is an iceroom , it's a labyrinth where the
    player can only move in one direction until he touch an obstacle.

    The multiplayer is a lobby type multiplayer where player will join a random lobby, choose the map and play together on the map (All map made can be played on multiplayer).
